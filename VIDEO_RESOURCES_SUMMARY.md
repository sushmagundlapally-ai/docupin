## üí° Pro Tips

### Recording Tips:
1. **Do a practice run** - Record once just to find issues
2. **Record in segments** - Easier to fix mistakes
3. **Slow down** - Speak 30% slower than normal
4. **Show, don't tell** - Demo > explaining

### Editing Tips:
1. **Cut silence** - Remove long pauses
2. **Add B-roll** - Insert slides during voiceover
3. **Use captions** - 80% watch muted on social media
4. **Music at 10-20%** - Subtle background only

### Presentation Tips:
1. **Hook in first 3 seconds** - Grab attention immediately
2. **One idea per slide** - Don't overcrowd
3. **Big text** - Readable on mobile
4. **Tell a story** - Problem ‚Üí Solution ‚Üí Impact
